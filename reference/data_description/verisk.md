# Verisk Data Documentation

Source: https://docs.deweydata.io/docs/verisk

## Overview

Verisk Marketing Solutions provides consumer and property data through the TrueSource Identity Graph, encompassing demographic data, location data, offline contact markers, Connex segmentation clusters, and Census fields.

## Available Datasets (via Dewey)

### 1. Consumer Address History
- **Description**: Up to 10 sequential addresses for millions of adult U.S. consumers
- **Use Cases**: Migration patterns, residential mobility research

### 2. Total Consumer Insights - Consumer Profiles
- **Description**: Hundreds of attributes on individuals and households
- **Coverage**:
  - Geographic data
  - Demographic data
  - Household composition
  - Real estate data
  - Segmentation clusters
  - Census-based indices

### 3. Total Consumer Insights - Property Data
- **Description**: Property-level information
- **Attributes**:
  - Property location
  - Ownership details
  - Construction year
  - Building dimensions
  - Materials
  - Condition metrics

### 4. Total Consumer Insights - Auto Ownership
- **Description**: Vehicle-related household information
- **Data Sources**: Licensed auto data
- **Features**:
  - Brand affinities
  - Vehicle type preferences
  - Intelligence models for vehicle assessment

## Data Foundation

Built on TrueSource Identity Graph:
- Demographic and location data
- Offline contact markers
- Connex segmentation clusters
- ~30 Census fields

## Research Applications

- Insurance underwriting analysis
- Consumer segmentation studies
- Public policy research
- Financial risk assessment
- Migration and mobility studies
- Property market analysis
